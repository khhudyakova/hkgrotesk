HK Grotesk is a sans serif typeface
inspired by the classic grotesques.
Geometry, metrics, punctuations
and OpenType features have been
updated to support a wide range
of projects such as environmental
signage, textface for books
and magazines, Interface,
Websites and Mobile Applications.
